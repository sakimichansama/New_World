Major animation works

君の名は

The film tells the story of male and female high school students meeting in their dreams and searching for each other

《君の名は》is an original Japanese animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai, with Kamiki Ryunosuke and Mone Kamishiraishi as the main voice actors. It was produced by Comix Wave Films and released in Japan on August 26, 2016. It was released in Chinese Mainland on December 2, 2016.

言葉の庭

The story originates from the "lonely and sorrowful" love story at the beginning of the Manyo collection, which reveals factors such as shoes, Manyo collection, Japanese gardens, rain...

《言葉の庭》Led by Makoto Shinkai, the film was simultaneously released in Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Japan on May 31, 2013. Main voice actors: Hanazawa Kana and Irino Miyu.

秒速5厘米

It is a short animation with three separate stories centered on a young boy, set in Japan from the 1990s to the modern day, showing the changes in Tokyo and other areas through the life of a young boy.

《秒速5厘米》Episode 1:《桜の書記》. Episode 2:《宇宙飛行士》. Episode 3:《秒速5厘米》.Main voice actors: Kenji Mizuhashi,Yoshimi Kondou,Satomi Hanamura,Aya Onoue.

天気の子

The movie tells a fantasy love story between a young man, Hidaka, and a young girl, Yona, who has the power to manipulate the weather.

《天気の子》 is an original animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai and produced by Comix Wave Films, which was released in Japan on July 19, 2019, and online in mainland China on July 21, 2020, where bilibili's Japanese version is a Blu-ray Disc source with subtitles that differ from the theater.

Personal Profile

Makoto Shinkai (新海誠), formerly known as Makoto Niitsu (新津誠).Born February 9, 1973 in Ogai-cho, Minamisaku-gun, Nagano Prefecture, Japan. Is a Japanese animation director, screenwriter, and manga writer, who graduated from Nozawa-Kita Higher School in Nagano Prefecture, Japan. From the Faculty of Literature, Department of Japanese Literature, Chuo University, Japan.

Personal Life

Makoto Shinkai's original name was Makoto Niitsu, and he was the son of a Japanese architectural family called the Niitsu Group. Makoto is married to actress Chieko Misaka, and they have a daughter, Seiji Niitsu.

Major Works

On March 3, 2007, he wrote and directed the animated romance film《秒速5厘米》, which was released in theaters and won the Best Animated Film Award at the Asia Pacific Film Festival.

On May 31, 2013, he wrote and directed the animated romance film《言葉の庭》, which was released in theaters and won the Most Outstanding Award in the Feature Film Category at the Stuttgart International Animated Film Festival.

In 2016, he wrote and directed the animated film《君の名は》, for which he won the Best Screenplay Award at the 40th Japan Film Academy Awards and the Best Director Award at the 26th Japan Film Critics Awards.

In 2018, he directed the animated feature film《天気の子》. In November 2019, this film won the Best Animated Film Award at the 44th Kochi Film Awards.
